FoodData Central Integration

VitalBites is proud to integrate with FoodData Central, the USDA's comprehensive database for detailed food and nutrient information. This feature allows our users to:

  • Search and browse a vast library of foods, beverages, and raw ingredients.
  • Access accurate nutritional details like calories, proteins, sugars, and vitamins.
  • Log meals effortlessly with auto-filled nutritional information.
  • Make informed decisions about their dietary habits and goals.

By leveraging FoodData Central, we ensure that VitalBites provides relaible, science-backed insights for a healthier you.

How It Works

When you search a food item using the FoodData integration, VitalBites fetches real time data using the FoodData Central API. Here's how you can get started:

  1. Enter the name of a food or ingredient in the search bar.
  2. Review the nutritional information returned from the database.
  3. And that's it!

This process is seamless, fast, and ensures that your nutrition tracking is up-to-date.

Data Accuracy and Reliability

FoodData Central is a trusted source developed by the USDA. It is continuously updated to include the latest research and information on food and nutrient composition. With VitalBites, you can rest assured knowing that your dietary data is accurate and reliable.
